What makes a good name

The most memorable media has a name made out of two words, if you choose only one word it might be mistaken by other thing that obviously already exists or in the case the word is made up, not a lot of people are going to remember it. More than 2 words is forgettable unless it's already something that is famous already. Usually a exception is when the game has the name of a relevant character (Zelda, Lisa, Omori...)

By two words I mean two nouns or adjectives, no connectors or pronouns. "The, Her, A, His..."

Perfect examples:

Mine+craft
Hollow+Knight
Under+Tale
Home+stuck
Final+Fantasy
Dragon+Quest
Pocket+Monsters
Fullmetal+Alchemist
