Art - Art style

Finding a new art style is hard...

Trying to find a easy to animate art style is hard, I've been going through a lot of styles to see what fits better, man, even the animation sheet I made 1 week ago is now useless! That had way too many details, and hard as fuck to draw with contrapposto and dynamism. This is very useful since it gives me a lot of room to play with the movement of everything and it's hella easy to animate

Now I am aiming for something like this maybe? Seems easy enough.

Notice how Apfel doesn't have such detailed eyes but still she has everything that characterizes her, and the cubism kind of style the body has lets me draw with contrapposto and dynamism much easier. (And if someone points out that the proportions are not right I can always say that it's a style LOL)

Notes so I don't forget...

Brush used: Frank Brush Pen India Ink Threshold; Size 30px
Shading: Color #efaab3 in multiply
