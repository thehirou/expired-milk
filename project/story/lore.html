Lore

Reference point: Mantana's Apocalypse (MA) -> Year 0

Year 5100 BMA:

  • Sinesperians sense an upcoming threat to their planet and decide to develop alchemy, after its creation, Sinesperia is divided into four major elements; water, fire, earth and air. Each one of them having a selected elemental warrior to fight the imminent threat, since they don't know when the threat is going to appear they wish to the Octagram necklace for reincarnation to be real so the chosen warriors can be present anytime to protect the planet.

Year 5001 BMA:

  • The first generation of warriors die. The next generation is reincarnated; Mantana, Pirodias, Aurora and Marina.

Year 5000 BMA:

  • The threat appears, a meteorite is headed right directly into Sinesperia, since the new generation of warriors have just been born they can't fight against the threat and fail. Almost all of Sinesperia gets extinct, the four warriors on the other hand, survive.

Year 4980 BMA:

  • The four warriors spend their lives investigating what could have happened to their land, they notice that other race of dwellers (Ceresians) are starting to habit their planet and they think that they were sent in that meteorite. They start looking for the Octagram necklace to get granted (not physical) immortality and lurk in the shadows of this new civilization so they can strike all them down at their lower point, just like they did.

Year 3000 BMA:

  • Pirodias dies, killed by a Ceresian. Reincarnates as the sun god.

Year 2000 BMA:

  • Aurora dies, killed by a Ceresian. Reincarnates as the wind god.

Year 1000 BMA:

  • Marina dies, killed by a Ceresian. Reincarnates as the ocean god.

  • (The reincarnations are not Sinesperians nor Ceresians anymore, they are protectors of the planet, that's what the reincarnation were for, to protect the planet even if they died. Their purpose wasn't to seek revenge.)

Year 13 BMA:

  • Blume is born.

Year 9 BMA:

  • Back-in-time-Apfel meets Blume, she gives her Kase. Blume then changes his name to Munchie.

Year 8 BMA:

  • Emilio loses his pregnant wife in a car crash.

Year 7 BMA:

  • Emilio starts researching about alchemy out of desperation so he can meet his deceased wife again, then he meets Mantana, who offers a deal to Emilio. Emilio has to give the octagram necklace to someone... let's say... "easily manipulable" so Mantana can take advantage and get granted one last wish. (Since Mantana already wished for something they can't get granted another one unless this procedure is met.)

Year 1 BMA:

  • After years of Mantana watching Emilio not letting go of the Octagram necklace they decide to posses him and give it to the next person he sees.

  • Emilio meets Blume for the first time, unwillingly gives the necklace to her.

  • Emilio and Blume feel a parental connection and start meeting more often, Emilio forgets about the necklace and the deal and decides to start living his life better, what he doesn't know is that he already gave the necklace to her.

Year 0 MA:

  • Emilio notices that he's missing the necklace, and when he meets with Blume, he notices that she has it, he takes it from her and runs away to meet Mantana wherever they are. Blume goes after him.

  • Emilio meets Mantana and tells them that he doesn't want the necklace anymore, that he doesn't want to keep with the deal, Mantana refuses and notices that Blume is also in the transmutation circle Mantana prepared, Mantana slashes Blume and kills her, the Mantana Apocalypse has begun.

  • Since everyone in the circle gets granted a wish (Mantana wasn't lying) Emilio wishes for REINCARNATION, this wish ends up being useless since reincarnation was the first wish ever wished thus it already exists, Mantana wishes for POWER so they can destroy the planet, Blume's soul wishes for PROTECTION so everyone she cares about in the circle gets protected (The moon moves towards the place they are, covering them from the meteorites). The apocalypse had already started but before it could go even worse Kase wished for STONE, to turn Mantana into stone and stop them.

  • Kase already knows about this, since he's a paradox entity, he knows that Apfel died in the battle against Mantana so he runs away looking for her. When he finds her lying on the ground he takes with him his earring. He also knows that she is supposed to be born from an apple tree so he starts looking for that tree.

  • Emilio wakes up after being unconscious in the ground, surrounded by purple flowers, the first thing he sees is a dead Blume and a post-apocalyptic planet.

  • Emilio takes Blume and makes a grave for her, next to an apple tree. Some days after, Kase finds the grave and patiently waits.

  • Write more about Emilio's journey.

Year 15 AMA:

  • Emilio notices that the lack of oxygen is starting to get noticeable and it's preventing him to live, Emilio dies.

  • Kase doesn't die because he's waiting next to Blume's grave, who grants him protection (the wish).

Year 700 AMA:

  • The turned-into-stone-Mantana decomposes so Mantana finally dies.

  • Mantana reincarnates as the protector of earth itself, bringing back to life flora to the planet.

  • This makes possible for fauna to appear in the next hundreds of years that would start re-inhabit the planet, the Akinomori forest being the place with most fauna concentration.

Year 710 AMA:

  • Mice start exiting the subway stations scattered around and begin to rehabite the forest. (This is why most of the forest is made out of mice.)

Year 1000 AMA:

  • Apfel is born.

Year 1018 AMA:

  • Apfel's journey to the mountains start!

  • Everything happens, she finds about her origins and goes back in time to stop Mantana.

Year 9 BMA (Alpha timeline):

  • Apfel defeats Mantana, then, Apfel dies and reincarnates into the unborn child of Emilio, the only one left that experienced the entire adventure was Kase, who will live forever and ever.

  • Mantana reincarnates as the protector of earth itself, bringing back to life flora to the planet.

  • The octagram necklace is lost, the paradox clock, broken in fight.
