Inspiration

All of this is what shaped what the Apfel Project is:

  • Attack on Titan:
    The first inspiration for the game, when it was only about discovering what was on the other side of the mountains. The feeling of mystery and lore that the walls and Paradis Island had inspired me a lot to make the mountains of Ceres.
  • Adventure Time:
    All the lore Adventure Time has and the worldbuilding shaped a lot the story and land, but the most influential part was definitely the "Adventure Time: Together Again" movie. That movie had an extremely big impact on how the story of this game would go, since it added the reincarnation aspect to this story.
  • The Legend of Zelda:
    Not as big of an impact as other but it reinforced the reincarnation aspect and some mechanics.
  • Homestuck:
    Thank Homestuck that the Apfel Project has some kind villain, before this, the only point of the story was to go to the top of the mountains, let Ili rest in peace and discover what the world had to offer beyond the mountains. Also, the post-apocalyptic and time travel part of the Apfel Project was shaped mainly by Homestuck.
    Also also, the animated scenes for the game were also inspired by the [S] Pages in Homestuck.
  • Final Fantasy 7:
    The main inspiration the Apfel Project has from Final Fantasy 7 is the music and graphics, the early story was also inspired by Final Fantasy 7 but ended up drifting away. Still, the post-apocalyptic part of Final Fantasy 7 (In the end of the game.) also shaped a little bit the worldbuilding.
  • Dragon Quest 8:
    Not a lot actually, just munchie, I really liked the concept of the protagonist having a mouse companion.
  • Fullmetal Alchemist:
    The fact that alchemy works here like it does in FMA is because it's inspired by FMA, basically it gave me inspiration to build the fantasy part of the story. The rules for the "magic", the lore, what can you do, how it works... Of course, I gave the rules my own touch and I made my own research about actual alchemy. This was dragged on from by previous cancelled game that gives name to the forest in the Apfel Project, Akinomori.
  • Mario & Luigi RPGs:
    I've only played Mario & Luigi, Partners in Time though. If I didn't have a dream reminding me how good that battle system was I wouldn't have even considered adding one inspired by it to the game.
  • AM Doctor by boun:
    I don't know why but something awakened inside my mind when I saw this animation, then, Apfel as a character was born, then I thought: "Why don't I make a game with this girl?"
  • This playlist:
    This playlist has a lot of songs I listened to and awoke something in my mind to change/add in the story of the Apfel Project.
