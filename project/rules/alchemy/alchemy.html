Alchemy

Alchemy was originally developed by the last Sinesperians in Ceres so they could use it against Ceresians, leaving traces and clues on how to use this ancient magic against them without them knowing. It works kinda like the lifestream from Final Fantasy 7, the Sinesperians being the Ancients.

Mantana, Pirodias, Aurora and Marina were the last Sinesperians in Ceres. In their long long lifetime, as previously mentioned before, they developed Alchemy, each one of them represented something, Mantana was Earth, Pirodias was Fire, Aurora was Air and Marina was Water. Everyone except Mantana were slain at some point, each one of them tried to get a Ceresian to work for them so they could use them as a gateway for destroying their planet as revenge. Mantana was the only one successful in doing that, she targeted Emilio, at least in the timeline where she is successful, obviously. (Timeline Apfel) ~ (Chaotic timeframe)

In real life, the rules of Alchemy are fairly simple, you get something after giving something of equal value. Socially this is something that is present everywhere, your bro asks you a favor and you help him out, in exchange you get, well, you improve your relationship with your bro. If you go to a bakery, you give 1$ and you get a loaf of bread. But when we want to talk about alchemy in the "magical" term there's a security wall to avoid accidental transmutations.

You need to call them with the scientific formula in order to proceed with the transmutation, that way you can avoid situations like imagine, casually having all the elements that compose the human body in one place and creating some weird creature by mistake.

There might be loopholes to this equal value exchange, but the only one known to man is a human soul, you sacrifice one person, and, if that one person was destined to live a long life, all the energy from its body gets expelled instantly, thus liberating enough lifestream to alchemize enormous results.

The one Emilio made with Mantana had one different detail, the philosopher's stone symbol, which would serve Mantana as a gateway to create a lifeform that would grant her anything she asked for if a human sacrifice was present in the transmutation, it's lifespan would be short so she had to be fast, one human soul has enormous quantities of energy, but still, not enough to create an immortal demigod.
