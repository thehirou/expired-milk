He decided that when Apfel turned 18 years old he would help her get to the top of the mountains.

The mouse Blume has is Kase, even though she named him Munchie. Kase was present the day Blume was slain so he could survive the end of the world to meet Apfel in the future, this is the outcome of Apfel failing the mission of stopping Emilio back in time, if Apfel is successful, Kase will live with Blume in the timeline where the world is saved.

That's why Kase usually wants to delay Apfel's adventure, he's scared of losing her so he's always trying to stop her, he wants to live in this timeline, he doesn't want to see Apfel die again, that's why he always kept a secret all of this, because he knows that Apfel is a curious girl and she would be even more interested in all of this.
