Vincent

Tags: #ceresian #npc

Detailed Info:

Present in Blume's timeline and in Apfel's as his reincarnation, he's basically Vincent Van Gogh. In Blume's timeline he's sad because he thinks he's a mediocre artist, in Apfel's timeline he's an aspiring painter that is influenced by paintings from his previous life not being aware, and is a fan of that mysterious painter from the past.
You can find him making a portrait for Ili, where he will be recreating his famous piece Skull of a Skeleton with Burning Cigarette

Inspirations:

Vincent Van Gogh because it's literally him.
