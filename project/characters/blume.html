Detailed Info:

Blume is a 13 year old girl, she lives almost everyday alone because her parents are not always at home. One day, while she was walking around town, she met Emilio, who was struggling with his groceries, so she decided to help him.

When they were at Emilio’s apartment, Blume showed interest in the alchemy experiments Emilio had laying around his home, after that, “Emilio gave” Blume an eight pointed star necklace (Octagram necklace) which she loved.

Blume loved spending time with Emilio, she felt like Emilio was the parental figure she lacked, so she started giving some visits to Emilio every now and then because Blume thought that he was feeling alone too, at first he was confused, but then they started enjoying spending time together, doing family things, like cooking spaghetti or shit.

One day, she tells Emilio that she forgot to thank him for giving her that necklace (Octagram necklace) the first day they met, Emilio, confused, says to her that he never gave her a necklace (Octagram necklace), Blume shows it to him, Emilio becomes terrified after seeing that Blume has the necklace (Octagram necklace) Mantana gave to Emilio to choose his sacrifice to start the alchemy process, Emilio connects all the dots and realizes that this is all Mantana’s doing, he takes the necklace (Octagram necklace) off her hands and runs over to where the alchemy transmutation was going to take place, Mantana is also there, waiting, Blume runs after Emilio, but he doesn’t notice. They arrive at the place, and basically, Mantana slays Blume and starts the transmutation.
