Detailed Info:

From a pre-prehistoric race, the Sinesperians. Human dwellers might have encountered some of these lurking in the world thus making them some kind of myth.

From the humans' perspective these creatures might have been mistaken for monsters even though they share a lot of physical features.

Mantana had 3 friends, each one representing one of the 4 elements, Fire, Water, Earth and Air. They're all dead, Mantana is Earth, now Mantana seeks revenge for their dead friends and wants to summon them in the transmutation circle to end the planet and hopefully reinstate them with Sinesperians. (Mantana fails in any possible outcome, since they "die(?)" either by Apfel in the Timeline Alpha or by Kase in the Timeline Apfel.)

About the bee thing, I had like, a fucking good idea, I don't want Mantana so be like, a literal representation of a bee but more like a metaphor, you see, Blume is the representation of a flower and Mantana is a bee... Now that I think of it this doesn't make sense because a bee is something beneficial to a flower, but you know, when a bee stings they die but still they infect with a fuck ton of poison their victim, I was thinking something like that happens when Mantana kills Blume, Mantana sacrifices themselves by killing Blume and still Mantana gets what they want... I... Don't know, now that I'm writing this down doesn't really make a lot of sense... Forget it

I'd like to redesign them a little more, Mantana looks way too bland, at first they were going to be fully black and white but with this new plot point of them getting turned into stone maybe some people won't notice the difference...
Commission someone to design them? Or just straight up design them.

Future design guides:

  • Goat-like.
  • Maybe deer-like???
  • Pitch black skin (like they're always under a shadow)
  • Hair color that represents their Earth aspect.
  • Complementary clothes (crop-top with a jacket over it, let the belly button be seen hehe)
  • Skirt? Or pants? Long? Short? Idk.
  • If possible, shades (sunglasses), they hate the sun. (Hard since their skin is pitch black.)
  • Their hair used to represent the Earth symbol and the messy pattern that looks similar to Apfel's gave away that they was the yin of the yang, so maybe keep that in mind for future design. (You have a lot of freedom with this one so surprise me.)
  • Of course, the Earth symbol has to be visible somewhere in the upper section of the clothing, wherever.
  • Sharp teeth (Like Terezi)
  • Should be slightly smaller than Apfel.
  • I'm not sure if Mantana has in any point of the game the Paradox clock so maybe that can be a accessory for them
  • Their legs should be shaped like a goat, not straight like human's.
  • (Remember, they're some kind of an alien so they shouldn't look too human-shaped, still, don't make them look like a "A Quiet Place" alien, I don't like referencing Undertale but keep in mind Toriel. Or even some creatures from Adventure Time.)
  • Speaking of Undertale, don't make them look like Asriel, Toriel, Asgore... The last thing I want is for people to tell me that both games have a goat-like villain.
  • Of course, a design easy to animate!! I have to animate a lot of scenes with Mantana in the shot, so don't add a thousand pockets in the jacket, or a million buttons... Just don't add a lot of things, keep it simple.
  • Wears a golden ring
