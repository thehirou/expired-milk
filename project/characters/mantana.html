From a pre-prehistoric race, the Sinesperians. Human dwellers might have encountered some of these lurking in the world thus making them some kind of myth.

From the humans' perspective these creatures might have been mistaken for monsters even though they share a lot of physical features.

They had 3 friends, each one representing one of the 4 elements, Fire, Water, Earth and Air. They're all dead and Mantana is Earth, now they seeks revenge for their dead friends and wants to summon them in the transmutation circle to end the world.
