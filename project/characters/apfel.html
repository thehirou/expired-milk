Detailed Info:

Apfel is a girl that was born from an apple tree, she’s energetic and very curious, her dream since she was born is to see what’s on the other side of the mountains that surround the place she lives in, she has a mouse friend, named Kase, he found Apfel laying next to that apple tree, hence her name, Apfel.

When she sleeps, she always dreams with a girl whose name is Blume, she doesn’t know it yet, but that’s her past life.

Once she discovers that Blume is actually her past life after finding a journal at her birthplace, Apfel’s journey shifts into discovering who Emilio is to know what his original intention of transferring Blume’s soul to Apfel was.

Somehow she has to go back in time to stop anything that might have caused this butterfly effect to save the world from its destruction.

Apfel goes back in time, where she stumbles across Blume, Apfel proceeds to give Kase to Blume, so she could take care of him, she gives back her distinct flower, Apfel puts it on her head. Blume might be around 8 years old at this point of the story.

Apfel says goodbye to Kase he gives her his earring, because if Apfel is going to stop whatever made Emilio transfer Blume’s soul into that apple tree, Apfel would have never existed.

Apfel dies, the world is not endangered anymore. Emilio’s wife doesn’t die, so she is able to give birth to a girl… Wonder who that girl might be? Alpha Apfel.
(Wait! Are you telling me that reincarnation existed all along?! Well yes, Emilio didn’t have a way to know if it existed or not, he was consumed by his existential crises and his faith, he was a scientist and he never would've thought that reincarnation existed. Maybe Mantana knew all along and he took advantage of his weakness.)

It makes sense that Apfel looks like Apfel, technically she was created by Emilio the first time. Emilio also names her daughter "Apfel", because he had a dream about it.

Years pass, Alpha Apfel meets Blume, Blume is 18, Alpha Apfel is 10, Kase obviously recognizes Apfel, but she doesn't. Blume tells Alpha Apfel that she looks like a girl she met 10 years ago who gave her this mouse.

(I know there’s a huge inconsistency about Kase being almost 30 years old now but let’s just let this one slide, this is EMOTIONAL, man!)
