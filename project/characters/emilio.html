Detailed Info:

Emilio is a scientist that lived in a world previous to Apfel. One day, he lost his wife and unborn daughter in a car crash because someone "tried to cross the road" at night. From that day he became depressed and started getting into alchemy, a science that would grant Emilio anything he wanted if he gave something of equal value in exchange.

What Emilio wanted was to make reincarnation possible, so he started studying the science of alchemy and started researching on how to accomplish such a wish.
Later he would meet the “Intermediary” (Mantana), a mysterious being that would guide Emilio in his research, he gives Emilio a necklace (Octagram necklace), who then Emilio has to give to someone he must sacrifice.

When Emilio finally manages to start the process of granting his wish, Mantana sabotages him and also gets the power, which he uses to destroy the world. Emilio manages to make reincarnation possible, also sending Blume's soul into an apple that he would later plant.

Emilio survives the Mantana apocalypse, writes the Alchemist Companion as a guide for Apfel and then MAYBE dies and reincarnates into Ili (Not remembering anything.)

At some point you will probably play as Emilio with the mission of planting the apple tree. Then maybe die? Idk, I would like for Emilio to explore himself a little bit, some character development.
