Ceres

Tags: #planet #place
Ceres is the planet where everything in this story takes place. Thousands of millions of years ago, this wouldn’t have been an habitable planet but mysteriously, at some point of its existence, 8 meteors impacted into its surface; a meteor of Fire, a meteor of ice (Water), a meteor of Earth, a meteor of Air, a meteor of Sulfur, a meteor of Mercury, a meteor of Salt and finally, a meteor of Spirit].

The impact points of these meteors were way too precise to be just mere coincidence, and what about the elements they were made from? This had probably been sent by something higher. In fact, the impacts were so precise that they formed an alchemy transmutation circle, which exchanged Ceres’ energy current to create life on this planet.

But in reality, before Ceres' creation exchange, life actually was able to emerge in Ceres, in fact, there were already dwellers, named Sinesperians. Almost every single one of them died after the collision of the 8 meteors that were thought to have collided out of pure randomness.

Some survived to tell the tale of the world and stayed hidden in the depths of Ceres, they were not enough to repopulate the planet, plus, 4 of the meteors that landed into Ceres were the key for new life to be created and evolve fast; sulfur, (Men, "Adam") and mercury, (Women, "Eve") were the key for life in the planet, while spirit and salt were useful for making the process faster, the rest of the meteors just gave shape to the world so it could vanish most vestiges of previous civilization.

The Sinesperians knew that somehow this had something to do with these new dwellers so they started planning revenge. After millions of years, only one of them stayed alive, "Mantana".
