Cola caps

Tags: #item
In the game there will be a certain number of vending machines, each one of them has a different kind of cola inside that you can obtain. Once you get to a secret part of the game you can exchange the bottle caps for music tracks in a record player, these music tracks are meant for invited composers. These are not stored individually in the inventory because it would take a lot of space, they will appear in some kind of collectibles menu.
