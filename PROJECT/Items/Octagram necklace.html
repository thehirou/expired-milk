Octagram necklace

Tags: #item
Necklace Mantana gave to Emilio who then gave to Blume which ended up in Emilio’s hands again and then Apfel’s. If Apfel is not successful in her back in time mission it will end up in Mantana’s hands starting the loop once again. So this item is a Paradox entity, it didn’t come from anywhere. This necklace works as a target point for Mantana to localize her victim and also has a function linked to the Paradox clock.
